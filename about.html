Compliance and Security

In the highly regulated healthcare industry, Compliance and Security plays a pivotal role. We ensure that our processes adhere to industry regulations, guaranteeing the security and confidentiality of sensitive healthcare data. Our commitment to security and compliance is unwavering, giving you peace of mind in an increasingly regulated industry. We take special measure to reduce data and security risks, including

  • Altering workflow
  • Providing compliance trainings for staff and vendors
  • Identifying and adopting compliance management protocols
  • Employing non-disclosure and confidentiality agreements
  • Creating emergency response and disaster recovery plan

Adhering to industry standard compliance, including

  • HITECH Act Compliance
  • HIPAA Compliance
